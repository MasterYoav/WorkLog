Privacy Policy - WorkLog

Last updated: February 2025

WorkLog is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Camera Access: Used only for uploading work-related photos or scanning QR codes. Images are not stored or shared without user consent.
  • Location Data (if used): May be collected to confirm employee attendance at specific sites. This data is not shared with third parties.
  • Authentication Data: Your login and profile information are securely managed via Supabase.

How We Use the Information

Data is used only to provide the WorkLog service – managing employee check-ins, work sessions, and task tracking.

Data Security

All data is transmitted over secure HTTPS connections and stored in a protected Supabase database.

Third-Party Services

WorkLog uses Supabase for data storage and authentication. Please refer to Supabase’s privacy policy for more information.
